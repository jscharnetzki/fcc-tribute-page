Steve Jobs' Life

1955 | Steve Jobs was born on 24th of February 1955 in San Francisco.

1975 | He started to work on Apple I Computer with Steve Wozinak. The first meeting in Gordon French's garage was on March 5, 1975.

1976 | On the 1st of April Apple Inc. was incorporated by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ron Wayne.

1984 | Macintosh is launched at Apple's annual shareholder meeting.

1985 | Steve Jobs got fired from Apple and started a new computer firm NeXT.

1991 | On March 18, 1991, Steve Jobs married Laurene Powel.

2000 | He returned to Apple as CEO at January 6, 2000.

2005 | Steve Jobs gave the announcement speech at Stanford University at May 17, 2005.

2009 | He took medical leave for six month and had surgery for liver transplantant.

2011 | On 5th of October 2011 Steve Jobs passed away.
